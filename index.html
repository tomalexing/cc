About

Impleum (https://impleum.com/) is a blockchain platform which has its basis on an open ecosystem of decentralized applications (DApps) in which it is being built. Data applications can also use the IMP coin.

Impleum advantages:

  1. The core of the team is specialists in the areas of blockchain and programming.
  2. Low reward for PoS. A limited and fairly small total supply of 100 million coins indicates that there will be no hyperinflation.
  3. The Impleum platform solves problem to use the blockchain system in company infrastructure using popular C# programming languages.
  4. Real use. DApps can to use the IMP coin. This leads to a broad distribution of coins among coin holders and their further shortages on market.
  5. Masternodes will allow to achieve faster and more private transactions.
  6. The project uses a modern application integration method with blockchain Impleum using the REST API.
  7. A large growing community of the Impleum project in messengers and social networks indicates a high level of trust in the project. All of the above indicates the huge potential of the Impleum project and gives reasons to believe that after entering the stock exchange IMP is waiting for a rapid takeoff prices. The IMP coin is scheduled for the end of the 2nd, beginning of the 3rd quarter of 2018. How to buy IMP now?

You can buy IMP on the service BTCIMP.trade. To do this you need:

  1. Register the address of your IMP wallet.
  2. The service automatically generates the BTC wallet address for the deposit.
  3. Send the required amount of BTC into the wallet address.
  4. IMP coins will be sent to a previously registered Impleum wallet address after confirmation of the transaction. The notice of purchase will come in History module.
