ABOUT IMPLEUM

Impleum is a blockchain platform which has its basis on an open ecosystem of decentralized applications (DApps) in which it is being built. Data applications can also use the IMPL coin.

Impleum advantages:

  1. The team's core consists of specialists in the area of blockchain and programming.
  2. Low reward for PoS. A limited and fairly small total supply of 100 million coins indicates that there will be no hyperinflation.
  3. The Impleum platform solves problem to use the blockchain system in company infrastructure using popular C# programming languages.
  4. Real use. DApps can to use the IMPL coin. This leads to a broad distribution of coins among coin holders and their further shortages on market.
  5. Masternodes will allow to achieve faster and more private transactions.
  6. The project uses a modern application integration method with blockchain Impleum using the REST API.
  7. A large growing community of the Impleum project in messengers and social networks indicates a high level of trust in the project.

All of the above indicates the huge potential of the Impleum project and gives reasons to believe that after entering the stock exchange, IMPL will see a rapid growth in price. The IMP coin listing is scheduled for the 3rd quarter of 2018.
